Harford Hall

0.00

Harford Hall is a three-story, coed, building that accommodates approximately 115 residents. All living quarters in this building are suites, which contain a living room, three to four bedrooms and one to three baths. Most academic buildings and libraries are less than a 10-minute walk away and the South Campus Dining Hall is only five minutes away.
