La Plata Hall

0.00

La Plata Hall is an eight-story, coed, traditional style hall that accommodates over 600 residents. Rooms range in size from single, double, triple and quad and each floor has a large community bathroom for residents to share. Located nearby are the football stadium, the Eppley Recreation Center, and the Stamp Student Union.
