Denton Hall

0.00

Denton Hall is an eight-story, coed, traditional-style hall that accommodates close to 542 residents. Rooms range in size from single, double, triple and quad and each floor has a large community bathroom for residents to share. Located nearby are the football stadium, the Campus Recreation Center, and the Stamp Student Union. The Dining Hall and a convenience store are only a few minutes away. Most academic buildings and libraries are less than a 15-minute walk away and the University Shuttle makes frequent stops in the community for overall safety and convenience.
