Prince Frederick Hall

0.00

Prince Frederick Hall houses 462 students in a combination of singles, doubles and four-person semi-suites. Approximately half of the residents are housed in traditional double rooms, while nearly 40% will be housed in four-person semi-suites. A small number of single and double rooms with private baths are available on each floor. All student rooms have space for a personal microwave and a refrigerator up to 3.6 cubic feet in size.
