Chestertown Hall

0.00

Chestertown Hall is a four-story, coed, traditional style hall that accommodates close to 126 residents. Rooms range in size from single to double and each floor has a large community bathroom for residents to share. Located nearby are Yahentamitsi Dining Hall, the football stadium, the Campus Recreation Center, and the Stamp Student Union.
