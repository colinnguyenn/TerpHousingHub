About

Our website contains on-campus and off-campus ratings and reviews that help other students select their future living space. There is an option to leave a review of your current living space so your voice can be heard. You will be able to see photos, real reviews, and ratings of each dorm or apartment at UMD.

Our Mission

Our mission was to create a website assisting the UMD population and future classes to see what dorms/campus housing was best for them. The significance of student housing cannot be underestimate, as it significantly impacts academic performance, personal development, and overall well-being. Inspired and similar to PlanetTerp, we aimed to create an accurate review system which included real students as well as utilizing social media to contribute to the ratings.
